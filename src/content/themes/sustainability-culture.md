A sustainability plan is only as strong as the daily behavior changes behind
it. Education, community events, and peer-to-peer learning turn residents,
businesses, and City staff from passive observers into active participants —
and that's the foundation every other section of the plan rests on.

**Local wins so far:** $25M in State funding secured to build a new community
center at Shorb Place — Alhambra's first dedicated community resilience center
and a hub for future sustainability programming. The Alhambra Library runs
gardening workshops; the City hosts community plant-swap events that promote
native and drought-tolerant species. The annual Eco Fair brings vendors,
exhibits, and the Sunday Farmers Market together on Main Street every spring.

**Community spotlight — the Annual Eco Fair:** Each spring, downtown Alhambra
becomes a celebration of environmental stewardship, organized by the
Environmental Sustainability Commission alongside the Sunday Farmers Market.
